Sure! Here are some resources that can help you prepare for interviews at product-based companies like Amazon, Facebook, Microsoft, and Atlassian:

Basic Programming Skills:

"Python for Everybody" by Dr. Charles Severance (Free online course): https://www.py4e.com/
"Java Programming and Software Engineering Fundamentals" by Duke University (Free online course): https://www.coursera.org/learn/java-programming
Advanced Programming Concepts:

"Algorithms, Part I" and "Algorithms, Part II" by Princeton University (Free online courses): https://www.coursera.org/learn/algorithms-part1, https://www.coursera.org/learn/algorithms-part2
"Introduction to Algorithms" by MIT (Free online course): https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/electrical-engineering-and-computer-science/6-006-introduction-to-algorithms-fall-2011/
Technical Interview Preparation:

"Cracking the Coding Interview" by Gayle Laakmann McDowell (Book): https://www.amazon.com/Cracking-Coding-Interview-Programming-Questions/dp/0984782850/

In addition to the resources above, you can also practice by solving problems on competitive programming platforms such as Codeforces, CodeChef, or Topcoder.
